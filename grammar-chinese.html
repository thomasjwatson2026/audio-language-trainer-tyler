Core Sentence Order

Subject + Time + Place + Verb + Object

Example: Wǒ jīntiān zài jiā chīfàn. (I eat at home today.)

No Verb Conjugation

Verbs usually do not change by subject or tense. Time words and context carry tense.

Question Particles

Add ma for yes/no questions.

Example: Nǐ qù ma?

Measure Words

Numbers often need measure words: yí ge rén, liǎng běn shū.

Common Pattern

shì...de emphasizes details.
