Core Sentence Order

Subject + Verb + Object (flexible with emphasis)

Example: Yo como comida.

Verb Conjugation Matters

Verbs change by person/tense: hablo, hablas, habla.

Gender & Number Agreement

Adjectives/articles agree with nouns: el chico alto, la chica alta.

Ser vs Estar

ser for identity/permanent traits, estar for state/location.

Common Tenses

Present, preterite, imperfect, future. Start with present + preterite.
